Dr. Alexander Coward

Hello everyone. Welcome to my website.

I spent my early career as an academic mathematician, working somewhere at the interface between pure mathematics and theoretical computer science. I also taught at universities including UC Davis, UC Berkeley, The University of Sydney, Thai Nguyen University, and The University of Oxford, which is where I did my undergrad and PhD.

In 2016 I left academia, at least in the formal sense, and became a startup founder/entrepreneur. I'm involved in a number of projects, some in a leading role, others in a supporting role.
